Grill Sandwich

Description:-

Ingredients

  • tomato
  • potato
  • cucumber
  • capsicum
  • onion
  • chutney
  • sev
  • chat masala
  • beetroot

Steps:-

  1. Steam or boil 1 medium beetroot and 2 small to medium potatoes in steamer or pressure cooker.
    If pressure cooking, then cook them with 1.5 to 2 cups water for 2 whistles.
  2. Chop the cucumber, tomatoes and onions in thin round slices. Peel and slice the beet and potatoes thinly.
  3. Using the bread slice of your choice (whole wheat, white or brown), spread a relatively liberal and even layer of butter on the inside slices of your bread.
  4. On one slice, layer rounds of potato. Sprinkle with a pinch or two of chaat masala and roasted cumin powder.
  5. Layer with tomato, onion, cucumber slices and beet slices (if using them). Add another pinch or two of chaat masala and roasted cumin powder, and finish with some black salt.
  6. Top with your other slice of bread, chutney side in. Spread a thin layer of butter on the outside layer of the top bread slice.
