Pizza

Ingredients

  • pizza base
  • pizza sauce
  • tomato puree
  • capsicum
  • mushrooms
  • onion
  • cheese
  • black olives
  • mozerella cheese
  • oregano
  • chilli flakes

Steps

  1. spread pizza sauce generously leaving the sides slightly on pizza base.
  2. also top with mushrooms, onions and capsicum.
  3. top jalapeños and black olives. adjust the quantity of jalapenos based on spice level.
  4. then spread generous amount of grated mozzarella cheese over the base.
  5. also sprinkle some mixed herbs or oregano or italian seasonings.
  6. brush olive oil over the sides of pizza crust to make pizza crust more crispy and golden brown.
  7. further, bake in a preheated oven at 500 degrees fahrenheit or 200 to 250 degree celsius for about 12 – 15 minutes.
  8. immediately slice the pizza.
  9. finally, serve veg pizza hot with chilli flakes and oregano on top.
